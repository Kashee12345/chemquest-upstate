HOW TO PLAY

🗺️ Tap a glowing site on the Upstate map to travel there. Drag, scroll, or use arrow keys to explore the map.

🏃 Each site's boss chases you down I-85! Answer 8 questions before the timer runs out. Correct = you pull ahead. Wrong = the boss gains a step.

💀 If your LEAD hits 0, you're caught. Outlast all 8 questions to defeat the boss.

🧪 Lifelines: 50:50 removes two wrong answers, ❄️ FREEZE blocks the boss's next step. Earn more every 3-streak!

🃏 Each boss defeated unlocks its Career Card: real local employers, real salaries, and the course path at USC Upstate.
